Glazed Donuts

The Fluffiest Glazed Donuts Recipe ! Homemade Glazed Donuts ( Doughnut Recipe ) Learn how to make donuts that taste just like krispy kreme donuts if not even better!

Link to original recipe: Melt In Your Mouth Glazed Donuts Recipe ( How to make the BEST Yeast Donuts ! ) Homemade Donuts


Recipe Difficulty Total Time Yield
Easy Prep Time: 20 minutes 16
Cook Time: 20 minutes

Ingredients

For Donuts
280 gr of All Purpose Flour ( 2 cups + 3 tbsp flour)
40 gr Sugar ( 3 tbsp)
1/2 tsp Salt
1/4 tsp Nutmeg
1 egg
120 gr Whole Milk ( 1/2 cup ) ( Every flour absorbs liquids differently, so you may need an extra 2-3 tbsp of milk)
40 gr melted Butter ( 1/3 stick )
7 gr Dry Yeast ( 1 packet )

For Glaze
250 gr Powdered Sugar ( 2 cups)
75 gr Butter ( 2/3 Stick )
3-5 tbsp Milk depending how thin you like your glaze
1 tsp Vanilla

Instructions
1. Add flour, nutmeg, sugar, salt, quick whisk to a bowl.

2. In a seperate bowl, add warm milk, egg, melted butter, and yeast.

3. Combine bowls, mix on high for 3 minutes.

4. On a flat oiled surface, knead for 10 minutes, transfer to oiled boil and cover and let rise

5. After first rise, flour the work area, four top of dough a little bit to pop bubbles, roll out dough, then cut out donuts.

6. Let donuts rise for about 20 minutes.

7. Fry donuts in 320-330 degree Fahrenheit. Fry both sides until beatiful golden brown.

8. Combine ingredients for glaze together in bowl.
